# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment for our community include:
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people.
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.
- Showing courtesy and patience.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
- Trolling, insulting or harassing comments, or inappropriate jokes.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing private information about a person without explicit permission.
- Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers at `contact@gophrql.org`. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Project maintainers are obligated to:
- Take all complaints seriously and respond promptly.
- Ensure confidentiality of the complainant and the accused.
- Take appropriate corrective action, which may include a warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban from the project.
